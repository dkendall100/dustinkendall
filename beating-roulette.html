Beating Roulette

  • Dustin Kendall

  • January 9th, 2019

Description

I lead my 4 person senior design team in the design of a system that takes positional input data from YoloV3, and used this data to train a neural network to predict likely outcomes of roulette.

We were awarded a grant to fund our project. Our required us to assemble physical hardware, set up realtime streaming hardware, collect and curate data, train YoloV3, train a predictive network, and collaborate between 5 people including our design advisor
